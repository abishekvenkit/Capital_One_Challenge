Trip Category vs. Passholder Type


    This graph shows us the relationship between pass type and trip type. The tall orange bars
    immediately tell us that one way trips are much more popular no matter what type of pass
    you hold. For monthly pass holders, there are almost 20 times more one way trips than round
    trips. Walk up users have a slighly lower ratio of one way to round trip. This makes sense
    because bike sharing is all about mobility. People can pick a bike from one place, and not
    worrying about returning it to the same location. If anything, they will take a different 
    bike back. Monthly users most likely ride bikes to school or work, drop it off, and take
    a different one back home. Walk up users may be slighltly more inclined to hold on to their
    bike while sightseeing or such. This is supported by our data.
