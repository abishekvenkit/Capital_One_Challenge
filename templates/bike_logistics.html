Popular Bike Stops (12 PM - 5 PM)

Amount of Trips Throughout the Day


    Above we can see the most used bike stations from 12 PM to 5 PM. This section of the
    day was chosen because, as we can see on the graph to the right, this is when the most rides
    are occuring and the most activity takes place. If we examine the location plot, we learn a 
    few things. During these peak hours, activity is very centralized in a couple locations. 
    The clusters on the plot with number 6 (meaning it is a top three location for all hours
    12 - 5 PM) is located right next to the California Department of Transportation. The clusters with
    4, (meaning it is very popular 4 out of the 6 chosen hours) are located near Wells Fargo Bank
    and Grand Central Market. In order to best support customers, bikes should be transported to these
    areas during the peak hours. In general, the heart of downtown in the most popular and should
    always have more bikes than the ourskirts.
